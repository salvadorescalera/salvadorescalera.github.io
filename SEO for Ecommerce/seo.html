Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

  • Improving these factors to boost rankings in a search engine like Google

    • Seo is an ongoing process of making improvements measuring the results and continue to improve on them.

  • Keywords
  • Meta description
  • Headings

  • Inbound links
  • Page titles
