Measurement and Optimization

Incorporating measurement and optimization tools

  • Using optimizely for A/B testing
  • Using services like optimizely helps determine different buying statistics depending on changing different aspects of your website.

    • Harnessing Google analytics to better understand site visitors

  • Google analytics is a powerful tool used to determine the circumstances that brought users to your website. Things like time language and location can be used to understand where people are coming from to your site.

    • Employing heat mapping tools to measure and monitor page usage

  • Heat mapping tools provide a guide to where users are most clicking on your website. This is valuable information used to better understand monitor, and tailor websites to your users likings.

    • Applying Moz for larger scale sites

  • Moz is a suite of SEO products needed to identify trouble spots in your website. The Moz suite has many tools used to determine where your site ranks among others using different aspects of your web page.
