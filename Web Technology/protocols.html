Internet Protocols

Protocols are simple languages that let computers share information with each other regardless of software or operating system.

The internet uses a group of protocols commonly referred to as The Internet Protocol Suite.

TCP/IP

  • Transmission Control Protocol and Internet Protocol.
  • Created in 1973, it has become the main protocol suite used to exchange data over the internet.
